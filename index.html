Hi, you have reached Andrea Reali personal page.
I invent, build and play devices to produce sounds and noises (read as music) and run workshops, sharing my knowledge with other people.
In 2014 I founded the art collective Audio HackLab.
You should have a look to the drone-noise boxes I produced under the name Kinetik Laboratories.
Most of my hardware projects have been released as open hardware and code is dedicated to the public domain.
