Hi, I'm Andrea. I invent, build and play devices to produce sounds and noises (read as music) and I run workshops, sharing my knowledge with other people.
In 2014 I founded the Audio HackLab artist collective.
You may be interested to the drone-noise boxes I produced from 2009 to 2018 using the name Kinetik Laboratories.
Most of my hardware projects have been released as open hardware and code is dedicated to the public domain.
