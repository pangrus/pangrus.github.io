After some years of use of the so-called social networks, I felt the desire to escape and create a place where the content cannot be capitalized by anybody but myself. I deleted my Facebook account and removed all my contents from Instagram and Twitter.
This is my digital garden, you are free to wander, without any definite purpose or destination.
It's written in HTML/CSS, using a text editor, mostly styled accordingly to the rules of Brutalist Web Design and A Manifesto for Preserving Content on the Web.
